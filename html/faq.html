Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Demon Slayer

General Questions

What is Demon Slayer about?

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba follows Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy who becomes a demon slayer after his family is slaughtered by demons and his younger sister Nezuko is turned into a demon. The story follows his journey to avenge his family and find a cure for Nezuko.

How many manga volumes are there?

The Demon Slayer manga consists of 23 volumes in total. The series was serialized in Weekly Shōnen Jump from February 2016 to May 2020.

How many anime seasons are there?

As of now, there are two main anime seasons and one movie:

  • Season 1 (26 episodes)
  • Mugen Train Movie (also adapted into a 7-episode TV version)
  • Entertainment District Arc (Season 2, 11 episodes)
  • Swordsmith Village Arc (Season 3, 11 episodes)

What is the correct watch order for the anime?

The recommended watch order is:

  1. Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba (Season 1, 26 episodes)
  2. Demon Slayer: Mugen Train (Movie or TV version episodes 1-7)
  3. Demon Slayer: Entertainment District Arc (Season 2, episodes 8-18)
  4. Demon Slayer: Swordsmith Village Arc (Season 3)

Who is the strongest Hashira?

Gyomei Himejima, the Stone Hashira, is generally considered the strongest of the current Hashira. He possesses immense physical strength and is the only Hashira who uses a weapon other than a sword (a spiked flail and axe combination).

Character Questions

Why doesn't Nezuko eat humans?

Despite being turned into a demon, Nezuko retains some of her human emotions and memories, which allows her to resist her demonic urges to consume human flesh. Instead, she sustains herself by sleeping and has developed the ability to shrink her body to conserve energy.

What is Zenitsu's fighting style?

Zenitsu Agatsuma uses Thunder Breathing (Kaminari no Kokyū), a swordsmanship style that mimics lightning. His signature technique is Thunderclap and Flash (Heiretsu Kaminari), an incredibly fast dash attack. Interestingly, Zenitsu can only use this technique effectively when he's unconscious or in a state of panic.

Why does Inosuke wear a boar's head?

Inosuke Hashibira was raised by boars in the mountains after his mother was killed by a demon. As a result, he developed a wild personality and wears the head of a boar to hide his "pretty" face, which he believes makes him look weak. The mask also serves as a reminder of his upbringing.

Manga vs Anime

Is the anime faithful to the manga?

Yes, the Demon Slayer anime is very faithful to the manga. The animation studio ufotable has done an excellent job adapting the source material, with only minor changes and additions (such as extended fight scenes) to enhance the viewing experience. The anime covers all major plot points and character developments from the manga.

How much of the manga has been adapted into anime?

As of 2023, the anime has adapted up to the Swordsmith Village Arc, which covers approximately the first 12 volumes of the manga (out of 23 total volumes). This means there's still about half of the manga left to be adapted into future anime seasons.
