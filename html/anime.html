About the Anime

The Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba anime television series is produced by ufotable and premiered in April 2019. The anime's stunning animation and faithful adaptation of the manga quickly made it one of the most popular anime series worldwide.

The first season covers the first seven volumes of the manga. The Mugen Train arc was adapted into a feature film that became the highest-grossing anime film and Japanese film of all time.

Subsequent arcs have been adapted into television seasons following the film's success.
