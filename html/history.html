History of Demon Slayer

Explore the rich history of the Demon Slayer Corps and the origins of demons

Historical Timeline

Heian Period (794-1185)

The origin of demons begins with Muzan Kibutsuji, who becomes the first demon and progenitor of all other demons. The Demon Slayer Corps is established to combat this threat.

Taisho Period (1912-1926)

The main story of Demon Slayer takes place during this era. Tanjiro Kamado's family is slaughtered by Muzan, and his sister Nezuko is turned into a demon.

Formation of Hashira

The strongest swordsmen in the Demon Slayer Corps are given the title of Hashira (Pillar). Each specializes in a different Breathing Style to combat demons.

Sun Breathing

The original Breathing Style created by the first Demon Slayer. All other Breathing Styles are derived from this technique.

Twelve Kizuki

Muzan creates the Twelve Kizuki (Twelve Demon Moons), consisting of Upper and Lower Ranks, to serve as his elite forces.

Final Battle

The Demon Slayer Corps launches an all-out assault on Muzan Kibutsuji and the Upper Rank demons in a climactic battle that will determine the fate of humanity.

Key Historical Figures

Muzan Kibutsuji

The first demon and progenitor of all other demons in existence. The primary antagonist of the series.

Yoriichi Tsugikuni

The strongest Demon Slayer in history and creator of Sun Breathing. Nearly killed Muzan in their first encounter.

Kagaya Ubuyashiki

The 97th leader of the Demon Slayer Corps. Despite his frail body, he provides strong leadership.
