Learn more about our mission and the team behind this project

Our Mission

The Demon Slayer Wiki was created by fans, for fans, with the goal of providing the most comprehensive and accurate information about the Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba universe.

Our mission is to create a welcoming community where fans can learn about the series, share their knowledge, and connect with other enthusiasts. We strive to maintain high-quality content that is well-researched, properly sourced, and regularly updated.

This is a non-profit project made out of love for the series. All content is created by volunteers who are passionate about Demon Slayer.

Wiki History

2019

The Demon Slayer Wiki was founded shortly after the anime adaptation premiered. Started as a small project with basic character pages.

2020

Grew to over 100 active contributors following the massive popularity of the Mugen Train movie.

2021

Completely redesigned the website to accommodate the growing content and user base.

2023

Reached over 1 million monthly visitors, becoming one of the most comprehensive Demon Slayer resources online.
